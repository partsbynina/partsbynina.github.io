Before Your Appointment

  • Deposit Policy:
    A non-refundable deposit is required to secure your appointment. Your deposit will be applied toward your final balance. Missed appointments and cancellations will result in the loss of your deposit.
  • Hair Preparation:
    Hair must be completely taken down before your appointment. Wash and blow-dry services are available for an additional fee if needed.
  • Style Requests:
    Please send a photo of your desired style before booking so pricing and appointment time can be confirmed.
  • Late Policy:
    Please arrive on time. Clients arriving more than 15 minutes late may need to reschedule depending on the day's schedule.
  • Pricing:
    Prices may vary based on hair length, density, size, and customization.
