Before Your Appointment

  • Deposit Policy:
    A deposit is required to secure your appointment. Deposits go toward your final balance and are non-refundable for cancellations or missed appointments.
  • Hair Preparation:
    Hair must be removed from previous styles before your appointment. Wash and blow dry services are available if needed.
  • Style Requests:
    Please send a picture of your desired style before booking so pricing and timing can be confirmed.
  • Late Policy:
    Please arrive on time. Late arrivals may affect your appointment time.
  • Pricing:
    Prices may vary depending on length, size, hair condition, and customization.
